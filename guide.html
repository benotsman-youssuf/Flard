How to Use Flard

Creating a New Section

  1. Click the button in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
  2. A new card will appear in the main container.
  3. Click on the card to open a popup dialog.
  4. In the popup dialog, enter a title for the section in the provided text area.
  5. Click the button to close the popup dialog. The card will now display the section title.

Adding Flashcards

  1. Click on an existing section card to open the popup dialog.
  2. Click the button in the bottom-right corner of the popup dialog.
  3. A new flashcard will appear in the popup dialog.
  4. Click the button on the front side of the flashcard to enter the question.
  5. Click the button to save the question.
  6. Click the button to flip the flashcard and enter the answer on the back side.
  7. Click the button to save the answer.
  8. You can add more flashcards by clicking the button again.

Editing Flashcards

  1. Click on an existing flashcard to flip it over.
  2. Click the button on the front or back side to enable editing mode.
  3. Make the desired changes to the question or answer.
  4. Click the button to save the changes.

Removing Flashcards

  1. Click on an existing flashcard to flip it over.
  2. Hover over the front side of the flashcard to reveal the button.
  3. Click the button to remove the flashcard from the section.

Changing the Theme

  1. Click the button in the top-left corner of the screen to toggle between light and dark themes.
